Our Story

Where Science Meets Passion

A legacy of academic excellence rooted in the heart of Dhaka

2009 Est.
Board Toppers Consistently producing district-level rankers

Newton Coaching Centre has been the cornerstone of science education in Dhaka, East Champaran since 2009 — a name synonymous with mathematical mastery and scientific thinking.

Founded by Er. Surendra Kumar, a Computer Science & Engineering graduate, Newton Coaching Centre was established with a single vision: to bridge the gap between classroom learning and competitive examination demands for science students.

Over 15+ years, we have mentored hundreds of students who have gone on to excel in board exams, entrance tests, and prestigious institutions. Our pedagogy is built on conceptual clarity, regular practice, and personalized attention.
